Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a complex developmental disorder that can cause problems with thinking, feeling, language, and the ability to relate to others

- American Psychiatric Association

Is your child missing milestones? Here are signs of non-neurotypical behavior:

Lining up toys

Outbursts of frustration

Stimming behaviors

Doesn't play with others

Spinning obsession

Difficulty communicating/Non-verbal

Parents' intuition

No eye contact

No communicative gestures
